Schedule & Pledges (Date-wise)

August 26 — Opening Day

  • Flash Mob — Surprise campus performance to kick off celebrations.
  • Inauguration of CSE FEST 2025 — Opening ceremony with chief guests and committee remarks.

August 27 — Prize & Farewell Day

  • Prize giving of RPC — Recognition for RPC winners and participants.
  • Prize giving of Sports — Awards ceremony for Sports Week champions.
  • Farewell of Batch 12 — A formal farewell for graduating students.
  • Farewell of Society Committee 2024 — Thank you and handover to the new committee.
  • Freshers Reception (Batch 15, 16, 17) — Welcome event for new students.
Note: Some detailed timings and stage assignments will be shared closer to the event — fixtures and precise schedules will be updated later.

Full pledge

By participating in CSE FEST 2025 Cultural events, participants agree to the following pledge:

  1. Respect fellow participants, audiences and venue staff.
  2. Abide by the event schedule and arrive at rehearsals on time.
  3. Follow safety and stage guidelines provided by organizers.
  4. Accept decisions of the organizing committee and judges.

The organizers reserve the right to modify the program for safety or operational reasons.
