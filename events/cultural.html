Participation Pledge

By participating in CoU CSE Fest 2025 Cultural events, participants agree to the following pledge:

1. Respect
Respect fellow participants, audiences and venue staff.
2. Punctuality
Abide by the event schedule and arrive at rehearsals on time.
3. Safety Guidelines
Follow safety and stage guidelines provided by organizers.
4. Accept Decisions
Accept decisions of the organizing committee and judges.
