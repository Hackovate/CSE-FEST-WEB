Overview

The Programming Contest at CSE FEST 2025 is designed for both individuals and teams. Expect a mix of algorithmic, data-structure, and optimization problems with time-limited rounds.

Format

  • Qualifiers (online)
  • On-site semifinals
  • Finals with live scoreboard

Rules & Languages

Allowed languages: C/C++, Java, Python. Standard contest rules apply; plagiarism and unfair collaboration will lead to disqualification.

Prizes & Perks

  • Top teams get trophies and gift vouchers
  • Mentorship sessions for finalists

Schedule

Qualifiers: 2025-10-20; On-site rounds: 2025-11-02
