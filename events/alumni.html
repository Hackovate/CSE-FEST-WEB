Alumni pledge

By joining the reunion, alumni agree to:

  1. Respect and support students during mentoring sessions.
  2. Share constructive feedback and professional insights.
  3. Uphold the event code of conduct.

How to Participate

  1. Register using the contact form or email the alumni committee.
  2. Select sessions you'd like to join (mentorship, panel, networking).
  3. Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in.
